About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Muhammed Muneef, a passionate mobile app developer dedicated to creating seamless and visually engaging digital experiences.

With 2+ years of experience in mobile development, I specialize in building cross-platform applications using Flutter and React Native. My journey began with a fascination for creating apps that solve real-world problems and enhance user experiences.

I believe in the power of clean code, intuitive design, and continuous learning. My expertise spans across:

  • Native iOS & Android Development
  • Cross-platform Solutions with Flutter
  • UI/UX Design & Prototyping
  • API Integration & Backend Services
  • App Store Optimization & Deployment

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring developers. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate on innovative projects.
